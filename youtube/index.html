Welcome to YouTube Cup Game

How to Play

  1. 1 Select a cup by sending your cup number in the chat.
  2. 2 You have 30 seconds to make your selection
  3. 3 If you select the cup with the BLACK ball, you advance to the next round
  1. 4 If you select the cup with the RED, you're eliminated
  2. 5 The last players standing wins!
  3. 6 You can replace your choice by sending another number before the countdown ends

Next game will start after the countdown ends >>>
